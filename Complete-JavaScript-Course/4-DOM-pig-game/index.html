Pig Game

Raise your round total by repeatedly rolling dice and adding your score together without rolling a 1, double 6's, or 2 totals of 6 in a row. Click the hold button to add your round score to the your total and end your turn.

If a 1 is rolled, the rolling player loses all round points and their turn ends.

If double sixes are rolled or a total roll 6 six is rolled twice in a row, the rolling player loses their whole score and their turn ends.
